Top features

Works with your contacts, regardless of your module.

By connecting to the default contacts module, we keep contacts updated with the latest call information and also updates contacts in Aircall so you can call from wherever works best for you. It does not matter if you use HR, CRM, Helpdesk, Field Service or any other module, your contacts will always be updated.

Updates your CRM opportunities

The integration offers specialized functionality for the CRM module by connecting to opportunities. It displays them in Aircall whenever you make or receive a call and also logs all call information in the opportunities once you are done with the call.

Updates your Helpdesk tickets

The integration also offers functionality for the Helpdesk module by connecting to Tickets. It displays them in Aircall whenever you make or receive a call and also logs all call information once you are done with the call.

Phone app embedded in Odoo

Thanks to click-to-dial functionality and our phone app being embedded in Odoo, you do not need to leave your main tool to make and receive calls. Perform all your needed activities from one place.

Other features

Select what numbers you want to connect

You can select what numbers should log in each module. That way you can have only your sales numbers logging to opportunities and your support ones logging to tickets.

Tag contacts

You can apply tags during any call and those tags will be applied to the native Odoo contact as native tags. You can use those later for reporting, flows, or any applicable Odoo feature related to tags.

Supports duplicated records

We all have duplicates in our lives. Our integrations are duplicate-friendly and allow you to select which contact and opportunities to log to.

Access call recordings and AI insights

If you have an Aircall AI package, you can access your call recordings, transcriptions, summaries, key topics, action items, and much more with just one click.

Unified call history

Access your call history in Odoo using the Aircall app.
