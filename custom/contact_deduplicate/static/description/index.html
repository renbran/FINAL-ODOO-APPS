🔍 Contact Deduplicate

Advanced Contact Deduplication Tool for Odoo 17

Eliminate duplicate contacts with intelligent detection and seamless merging capabilities.

🎯Intelligent Detection

Uses advanced fuzzy matching algorithms to identify potential duplicates based on names, emails, phones, addresses, and VAT numbers with configurable similarity thresholds.

🔄Flexible Merging

Three merge strategies: Keep master data, intelligent data consolidation, or manual field selection. Preview changes before merging with comprehensive field mapping.

📊Complete Management

Track duplicate status, maintain merge history, perform batch operations, and manage the entire deduplication workflow from a centralized interface.

🔗Related Records

Automatically migrates all related records (sales orders, invoices, CRM leads, etc.) to the master contact during merge operations.

⚙️Configurable Rules

Customize detection sensitivity, field weights, and similarity thresholds through system parameters to match your business requirements.

🎨User-Friendly Interface

Seamlessly integrated into existing contact forms with intuitive buttons, clear status indicators, and comprehensive duplicate management views.
