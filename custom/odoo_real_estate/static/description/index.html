Odoo Real Estate

Odoo Community Real Estate Management App

Overview

The Real Estate Management module is designed to help real estate agencies, brokers, and property managers efficiently handle every aspect of property and client management within Odoo. It provides a structured system to manage property listings, track transaction stages, and store client information in a centralized database.

With intuitive interfaces and seamless integration into Odoo, users can manage sales and rental processes, assign agents, monitor progress, and generate insights through reports. This module brings transparency, organization, and improved workflow to your real estate operations.
